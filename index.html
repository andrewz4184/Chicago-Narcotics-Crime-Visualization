Narcotics Crime Concentration in Chicago

Drag on the timeline to select a time range and filter the map. Double-click to clear the selection.

Dataset

Chicago Police Department CLEAR dataset. Narcotics crimes from 2001 to present. District boundaries from City of Chicago Data Portal.

Interactions

Brushing and linking: Drag on the timeline to select a time range; the choropleth updates to show narcotics crime concentration for that period.

Tooltips: Hover over a district to see its number and crime count.

Highlighting: Hovering highlights the district and dims others.
